Our Story

Inspired by our French heritage, Carte d'Or sparks wonders for dessert lovers all over the world. From a decadent single scoop to a dazzling Chocolate Dacquoise, the possibilities of gourmet desserts are endless, thanks to the inventive range of Carte d'Or flavours. With Carte d'Or, it's more than just ice cream. It's artistry, it's creation, it's imagination. All at the hands of one expertly crafted, single scoop.

Taste Always Comes First

: Hand-pollinated, hand-picked and traditionally cured vanilla beans from Madagascar,
which gives our Vanilla ice cream its classic rich flavour.

: Some of the finest cocoa in the world from Ecuador, often referred to as “black gold” by locals of the area. It’s also Rainforest Alliance Certified.
It’s this cocoa that gives the dark chocolate pieces in our Indulgent Chocolate their rich chocolate taste.
